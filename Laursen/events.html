We have a lot of events that happen here with AWS. We make metal roses, have opening and closing socials, and other activities throughout the semester.

Here at BYU-I we have a great community where we learn, grow, and socialize with each other. Every activity we have a great time with laughs and activities. The environment at the social and in the weld lab makes you feel included and cared for. In the Welding Engineering Technology degree, all the professors generally care for you and want to see you succeed in this life.

A reminder we have a forum every Thursday (if things don't conflict with the time) @ 11:30 am in the Austin ROOM 202 where we have treats and devotionals where classmates and other students are able to share their internship opportunity and/or cool and fascinating facts about welding.

Also, on Thursday evening @ 7:00pm we have Weld night in the weld lab in the Austin Building where we demonstrate new welding processes we don't see much in class and you can work on personal projects. The fee for the metal and gas used is $10 per semester.

Click the RSVP button to see what events are happening and be part of the AWS community here at BYU-I.
